Why It Matters

The number of armed conflicts in the 21st century has grown drastically, resulting in many being killed and record-shattering levels of forced displacement. These conflicts are not limited to a battlefield, rather they are bleeding into urban areas where civilians are increasingly bearing the risks of the battle. Being aware of where these conflicts are concentrated is the first step to advocating for more responsible social policies and humane immigration policies.
